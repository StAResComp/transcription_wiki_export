Possibly: An accurate alphabetical index of the registered entails in Scotland, from the passing of an act of Parliament in the year 1685, to February 4. 1784. Containing the number of the entail as it stands on record; the volume; the folio; date of the entail; date of registration; entailers names and the leading lands; with the shires in which all the lands lie. By Samuel Shaw, writer in Edinburgh. (Edinburgh : Printed for the compiler, MDCCLXXXIV. [1784]). http://library.st-andrews.ac.uk/record=b2046909~S1
