Possibly: A Letter from Rome, Shewing an exact Conformity between Popery and Paganism: or, The Religion of the Present Romans, derived from that of their Heathen Ancestors. To which are added, I. A Prefatory Discourse, containing an Answer to all the Objections of the Writer of a Popish Book, intituled, The Catholic Christian instructed, &c. with many new Facts and Testimonies, in farther confirmatin of the general Argument of the Letter. And II. A Postscript, in which Mr. Warbuton's Opinion concerning the Paganism of Rome is particularly considered. By Conyers Middleton, D.D. Principal Librarian of the University of Cambridge.
