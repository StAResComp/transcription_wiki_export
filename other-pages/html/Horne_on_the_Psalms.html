Possibly: A commentary on the book of Psalms. In which their literal or historical sense, as they relate to King David, and the people of Israel, is illustrated; and their application to Messiah, to the Church, and to individuals, as members thereof, is pointed out: with a view to render the use of the psalter pleasing and profitable to all orders and degrees of Christians. By George Horne, D. D. President of Magdalen College, Oxford, and Chaplain in Ordinary to His Majesty. 2v. (Oxford : At the Clarendon Press, M DCC LXXXIV.
