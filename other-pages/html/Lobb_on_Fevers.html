Possibly: Rational Methods of curing fevers: deduced from the structure, and oeconomy of human bodies, and the different states of the solids, and fluids, under the different classes of fevers. With practical rules deduced from them, shewing in what cases they are proper, and may be beneficial ; and in what, they are improper, and will be hurtful.
