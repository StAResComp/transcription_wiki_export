Secondly, An Account of all the Diseases incident to Horses, with their Signs, Causes, and Methods of Cure ; wherein many Defects in the Farriers Practice, are now carefully supply'd, their Errors expos'd and amended, and the Art greatly improv'd and advance'd, according to the latest Discoveries.
