Possibly: A Critical Dissertation on the Book of Job, wherein the account given of that book by the author of The Divine Legation of Moses Demonstrated, &c. is particularly considered; the antiquity of the book vindicated; the great text (Chap. xix. 25--) explained; and a future state shewn to have been the popular belief of the ancient Jews or Hebrews. By Charles Peters, A.M. Rector of St. Mabyn, Cornwall. (London : Printed for W. Johnston, in St. Paul's Church-yard; and P. Davey and B. Law, in Ave-Mary-Lane, M DCC LVII.
